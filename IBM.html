Cyber Security Specialist

Roles and Responisibilities

  • IT Security consultant for SOC Solutions to delivery security control to customer Infrastructure
  • Assistance/support for implementation of security Solutions as QRadar SIEM, Deception, Anti-APT.
  • Automation of tasks using Python scripting.
  • Interact with internal IT teams for requirements gathering, approach discussion, and delivery
  • Administration, implementation, integration of security logs from infrastructure layer and security device using the SIEM tool.
  • Working in adherence to regulatory compliance, i.e. RBI, External and internal audit for measuring the current compliance levels.
  • Responsible for correlation rule on SIEM to manage Security incidents as per customer policy
  • Integration of various log sources and analysis of events.
  • Revise and develop process to strengthen the current security operational framework.
  • Supervision of Activity by L1, L2, Team Hiring.
  • Analyse alerts to qualify them to ascertain if they are false positives or incidents requiring response.
  • Ability to detect cyber-attack and  respond to incidents in a timely fashion .
  • Study log formats and  interpret them with a view to identify and detect suspicious activity.
  • Creating Queries, Filters, Reports, and  Rules .
  • Work interactively IT and security tools teams to ensure incidents are mitigated or responded to in a timely manner.
  • Support the IT teams during major incidents and work closely to ensure effective response and mitigation. Generate RCA reports to institute a process of learning from incidents.
  • Work closely with tools teams to fine tune alerts for reduction of false positives.
